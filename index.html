About the Project

Our Water Level Management System is designed to address the challenges of water scarcity and flooding by providing a reliable and efficient way to monitor and manage water levels.

Goals and Objectives:

  • Ensure optimal water usage
  • Prevent water wastage
  • Provide early warnings for potential floods
  • Facilitate data-driven decision making

Features

Real-time Monitoring

Continuously tracks water levels in reservoirs, tanks, and other water bodies, providing instant updates on the current water status.

Alerts and Notifications

Sends alerts via SMS and email when water levels reach critical thresholds. Customizable alert settings are available.

Historical Data Analysis

Stores and analyzes historical water level data, helping to understand trends and make informed decisions.

Integration

Easily integrates with existing water management and control systems, supporting various communication protocols.

How It Works

Our system is based on modern IoT technologies and consists of the following components:

  • Sensors: High-precision water level sensors installed at various points.
  • Gateway: Collects data from sensors and transmits it to the cloud.
  • Cloud Platform: Processes and stores the data, providing analytics and visualizations.
  • User Interface: Web and mobile applications for real-time monitoring and management.
